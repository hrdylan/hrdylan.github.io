DJ Hero

What we do

DJ Hero is a platform designed connect people musically. Our goal is to integrate the tastes of party atendees with the songs they hear at parties. By allowing hosts to curate guest lists and guests in turn to anonymously express music preference, this app allows DJs to make the perfect set list while exposing everyone to new tracks in the process.
