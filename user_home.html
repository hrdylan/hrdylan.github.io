    Adding Music Preferences
    On the 'My Party Songs' tab, you have the opportunity to add a number of songs that you would like to hear at parties. Simply look up a song in the search bar, and click it to have it added to your preference playlist. Our database currently has a number of classic rock hits, so try adding a song by The Beatles! Once you're satisfied with your choices, click the Save Playlist button. Then, next time a host puts you down as someone attending their party, they will see what kind music you'd like to hear. All reports to hosts are aggregated and anonymous, so no need to worry about being singled out. Party on!
